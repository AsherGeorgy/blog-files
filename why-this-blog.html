Is Social Media all bad?

Absolutely not.

Many use social media to grow their businesses, expand professional networks, raise awareness about important issues, and stay connected with loved ones. Social comparisons can motivate individuals to improve, set goals, and strive for excellence. Following successful people can inspire and encourage personal growth.

That said, it is important to remember that everyone is on their own journey with their own ups and downs.

  • Not everyone began from the same starting point or were given the same opportunities.
  • Always take social media with a grain of salt. While some may be genuinely doing well, many are likely exaggerating or straight up faking it.
  • Nobody has everything about their life exactly figured out and perfectly lined up.
  • Live life steadily with a longer term perspective. Rome was built one brick at a time.

Take a step back and be grateful for the blessings in your life. For perspective, according to CNBC, if you have $4,210 (‚Çπ3,50,000) to your name, you belong in the top 50% of the world, i.e. more than half the world wishes they were in your position financially.
